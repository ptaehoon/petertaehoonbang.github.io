Barvel

An Andriod app, designed for customers to easily order drinks in the bar with phone.


Motivation of this app?

We, as a college student, like to have fun after studying either in school to chill, or to go out to party. I don't personally enjoy drinking alcohol, but as a group, we decided to do something that relate to partying and drinking. In the end, we decided to choose a bar app since many apps are already developed in regular restaurant and we wanted to explore designing app for a bar. We understood that in the bar, it tends to get crowded with so much noise. So we thought that instead of ordering to the bartender, why not developed an app for customers to easily order whatever they want?

How the app was developed

This app was developed with team of five. All coded with Andriod Studio, our group worked on User Interface, Simple App Features, and Better Quality. Our group significantly focused on simplificaiton of app features and easily recognizable text in consideration of bar customers to be drunk and lazy to order.

Features?

Simplification of the app was our goal. In the app, customers get the main menu page, they can choose either drinks or foods. They can choose a farvorite menu or go to history to see what they have ordered before. In the drinks section, customers can choose list of drinks such as beer, cocktail, wine, etc. They have the option of choosing how much ice they can get, number of drinks, whether they want lime, etc. After ordering, they will get the popup notificaiton to pick the drink.

Green Hats

Created a Mixed Reality (MR) that users can interactively pickup trash objects to save endangered animals. Established an application in order to spread awareness of recycling in order to save wild animals.


How we came up with the app?

Our group and I were very confused on what to make for our MR application. Our group initially wanted to do topic that related to animals. However, I knew that animal topic could be a broad topic that will be hard to implement in the MR. Therefore, I proposed to relate animals with environmental issues. Which we decided to do "recycling" topic to save endangered animals.


What kind of game?

Our group initially thought to make an animal game that had diverse features. Such features include animals dying after consuming trash, endangered animals ability to reproduce if no trash are located near them, etc. However, knowing that implementing these features are nearly impossible, my group and I proposed to stick in to one major theme. I told that we should do something that we can implement in the function. In the end, our group decided to make an MR that emphasized on recycling to save endangered animals.

StoryBoard



Features?

Trash is auto-generated in random places.
Tigers are auto-generated every 40 seconds. (Initially only three)
Tiger dies when it is near the trash for 10 seconds.
You can collect the trash by gazing the trash object for 0.5 seconds.
Tigers will stay or move around.

Human Robot

The project's task was to identify a scenario a where a physical robot can provide service before, after, during the super thypoon in Hong Kong.


Different Scale of Thypoon

When coding the Robot, our team of coders tried to code robot by different thypoon level. When minimal thypoon is signaled, just alert system will be returned. However, when high rising thypoon is alerted, then the robot will be more active alerting the people, playing music for comfort, telling nearby safety shelters.


Scenario?

The first scenario that human robot is necessary was when older generations could possibly have hard time using internet. Human robot can basically tell elders that storm is approaching what excactly elders must do. If they need to go to shelters or whether they should be fine staying home. Human robot will assist them.
The second scenario is when just in general everyone would need. For example, a parent wanted to get food before huge storm. However, parent didn't know where and how safe it is right now to go out. The human robot will basically assist her and where exactly to get food supplies.


Contribution?

Group of team member worked on what to insert on the functionalities of human robot.
Each Level had differnt functions
Level 1: List how the thypoon is approaching and remind the people about it
Level 3: Show near my supplies based on what the user clicked for what he or she needs
Level 8: Make sure person is safe and play assaging music to calm people down
