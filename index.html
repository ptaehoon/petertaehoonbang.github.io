Barvel

An Andriod app, designed for customers to easily order drinks in the bar with phone.


Motivation of this app?

We, as a college student, like to have fun after studying either in school to chill, or to go out to party. I don't personally enjoy drinking alcohol, but as a group, we decided to do something that relate to partying and drinking. In the end, we decided to choose a bar app since many apps are already developed in regular restaurant and we wanted to explore designing app for a bar. We understood that in the bar, it tends to get crowded with so much noise. So we thought that instead of ordering to the bartender, why not developed an app for customers to easily order whatever they want?

How the app was developed

This app was developed with team of five. All coded with Andriod Studio, our group worked on User Interface, Simple App Features, and Better Quality. Our group significantly focused on simplificaiton of app features and easily recognizable text in consideration of bar customers to be drunk and lazy to order.

Features?

Simplification of the app was our goal. In the app, customers get the main menu page, they can choose either drinks or foods. They can choose a farvorite menu or go to history to see what they have ordered before. In the drinks section, customers can choose list of drinks such as beer, cocktail, wine, etc. They have the option of choosing how much ice they can get, number of drinks, whether they want lime, etc. After ordering, they will get the popup notificaiton to pick the drink.
